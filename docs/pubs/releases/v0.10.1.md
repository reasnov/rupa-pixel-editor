**v0.10.1** focuses on structural integrity, ergonomic refinement, and workflow fluidity. This update solidifies the **Global Canvas Standard**, ensuring that every frame and layer in your project breathes the same dimensions, while introducing smart tools to make workspace adjustments effortless.

### Smart Math Inputs
The **Sanctuary Settings** now support arithmetic expressions in dimension fields. You can now perform quick calculations like `32*2` or `16+8` directly in the width and height inputs.

### Aspect Ratio Lock (üîí)
Maintain your project's proportions with the new **Ratio Lock**. When active, adjusting one dimension automatically calculates and updates the other, keeping your composition consistent.

The **CUT**, **COPY**, and **PASTE** operations are now strictly layer-aware. 
- **Copy/Cut** now harvests pixels only from the active layer.
- **Paste** now merges data seamlessly into the current layer and tracks the entire operation in the **History Stack (Undo/Redo)**.
